Ode to my mother

Rabindranath Nath Tagore says "I cannot remember my mother, only when from my bedroom window I send my eyes into the blue of the distant sky, I feel that the stillness of my mother's gaze on my face has spread all over the sky." [Read the original Bengali poem (Remembrance) here: https://www.bangla-kobita.com/rabindranathtagore/mone-pora/]

The utterance of the word MOM Brings before my eyes the face of a woman. She is the most beautiful woman in the world, Possessing a remarkable character. No one can take her place in this world.

God has created mothers because He himself cannot take care of all his children at the same time She is the woman who can solve any types of problems at one go. She is a great cook, A full-fledged gardener, An ideal mother And that is why she is my mother.

She is the one who wipes away my tears When I am sad and share all my problems, sorrows And she is never tired of doing all this.

She is the person who wakes up early in the morning, Before anyone does And goes to sleep after everyone, She does all the household work. God perhaps has gifted her with All the energy to do work, She gets very little time to spend on herself

And because of all these, she is a mother My lovely mother

As a daughter, I praise her very little, She is my mother and only my mother.

May God bless her and give her all the happiness of her life.
